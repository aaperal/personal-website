Me

Hello! My name is Alan Peral and I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of California, Santa Cruz. I am interested in data visualization, human computation, crowdsourcing and collaboration, games with a purpose, and things that fall within the vast realm of "tech for good." Here is a short list of papers that fascinate me (not exhaustive):

  • Labeling Images with a Computer Game
  • Participation and Publics: Supporting Community Engagement
  • The Anatomy of a Large-Scale Social Search Engine
  • Twitch Crowdsourcing: Crowd Contributions in Short Bursts of Time
I also like making videos and exploring creative storytelling endeavours. Check out a few of my videos here. Other people have made amazing explore explanations, melding art with education to make captivating experiences. When I'm not working or doing my homework, I can be found playing soccer or video games, getting involved in community activties (volunteer and otherwise), dancing, or walking my dog.
